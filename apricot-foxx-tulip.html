Types of Tulips

Apricot Foxx Tulip

Appearance

Theses tulips have a tulips with a pink center and peachy yellow outer color.

Height

Can grow to be around 18 inches tall.

Vase Life

Usually last about one week in a vase.

Planting Time

These tulips are best planted around late autumn before the first frost.

What I Like About These Tulips

  • very springy
  • unique color pattern
  • smooth and silky petals
